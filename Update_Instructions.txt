- Instructions on how to perform the update are as follows, 
    1. Download and Extract the upgrade file. 
    2. Open the folder called "Upgrade" and Copy all the files inside and replace them to your C4G BLIS folder. 
    3. Run update_C4GBLIS_v3.2.bat to start the update process. 
    4. Wait for C4G BLIS to startup. Click on the update button on the home page.
    
    5. Once the update completes, you can continue to use the system.
